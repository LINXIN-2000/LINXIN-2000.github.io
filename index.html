LIN Xin

LIN Xin is an undergraduate student in the Experimental Media Class at the School of Journalism and Communication, Nanjing University. He is a resident research student at the Institute for Advanced Studies in Humanities and Social Sciences (IAS), Nanjing University, focusing on Jewish scriptures and history directed by Professor Li-hong Song. He served as the dean of the Institute for Readings and Studies in Humanities and Sciences (RIS), Nanjing University, an organization that can connect with scholars on and off campus to offer small academic seminars for undergraduates of 15-25 students, for which participants receive credit and courses are funded.
